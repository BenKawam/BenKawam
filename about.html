I am a behavioural ecologist, (soon) starting a PhD with Julia Ostner and Oliver Schülke at the German Primate Center and the University of Göttingen. My PhD research will address how between-group competition and within-group relatedness interact to shape social structure in wild macaques. I am generally obsessed with primates, and incidentally developed interests for statistical inference, reproducible science and, recently, digital illustration.
